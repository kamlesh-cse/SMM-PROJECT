AMAZON TOS COMPLIANCE RULES

✅

Request reviews for one order only

Each email must reference a specific order. Bulk review solicitation is prohibited.

✅

No incentivised reviews

Never offer discounts, refunds, or gifts in exchange for reviews. Strictly prohibited.

✅

No directing to only positive reviews

You cannot ask buyers to leave a "positive" or "5-star" review. Neutral language only.

✅

Use Amazon's approved channels

All buyer-seller messages must go through Amazon Buyer-Seller Messaging or the "Request a Review" button.
