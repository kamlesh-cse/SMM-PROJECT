MOBILE MARKETING TACTICS

Six areas where mobile-optimisation makes the biggest difference for Amazon sellers.

🖼️

Mobile Image Stack

Your 7 product images must read clearly on a 390px screen. First image and infographics need large text, bold icons, minimal clutter.

📝

Bullet Point Formatting

On mobile, Amazon shows only the first 2–3 bullets above the fold. Lead with your biggest benefit in the very first bullet point.

🎬

Vertical Video Ads

9:16 format video ads for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts. The majority of social video is consumed in portrait mode.

📲

Amazon App Push Notifications

Amazon's Brand Follow sends push notifications to followers when you go live, drop a deal, or post new content.

🔗

Deep Link Attribution

Use Amazon Attribution deep links in your social bio to track which mobile social post drove each purchase on Amazon.

⭐

Mobile Review Prompts

Request a Review triggers inside the Amazon app — shoppers are 3× more likely to review from their phone than desktop.

THE AMAZON APP ADVANTAGE

Over 98 million Americans are Amazon Prime members, and most access Amazon exclusively through the mobile app. Your brand needs a mobile-first strategy to capture this audience where they spend their time.

  • Amazon Live streaming reaches app users with real-time shoppable video
  • Brand Posts appear in the app's dedicated Posts feed (like Instagram for Amazon)
  • Brand Follow notifications are push alerts — the highest-intent marketing channel
  • Subscribe & Save is predominantly completed on mobile
  • Amazon Moments delivers rewards inside the app to drive loyalty
